Absolutely. Every event hosted by Mistress Hellkitty enforces a strict aesthetic standard. Specifics vary by event (see ticket description), but effort is mandatory. Those failing to adhere to the code will be denied entry without refund.

We operate under a zero-tolerance policy regarding non-consensual behavior. While the themes are intense, safety is paramount. Any breach of protocol results in an immediate ban from all future events.

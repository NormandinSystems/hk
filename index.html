The Production

Who We Are

Curating Fantasy

Hellkitty Studio Events is Montreal's premier independent production house dedicated to the avant-garde. We are not a venue; we are the architects of the evening.

We partner with historic theaters, hidden warehouses, and luxury spaces to create ephemeral worlds. From masquerade balls to neo-burlesque showcases, our events are designed for those who view nightlife as a participatory art form.
